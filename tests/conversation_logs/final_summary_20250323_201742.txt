FINAL SUMMARY AND ACTION PLAN
==================================================

During the coaching session, the client expressed frustration with the lack of growth opportunities in their current role and a desire to move into a leadership position. Despite seeking more responsibility, their manager advised them to wait. The coach suggested exploring options for developing leadership skills outside of work, such as volunteering or taking courses.

Actionable Steps:
1. Research and sign up for leadership development courses or workshops to gain relevant skills.
2. Look for volunteer opportunities that allow you to practice leadership and decision-making.
3. Network with professionals in leadership roles to gain insights and mentorship.
4. Keep track of your progress and accomplishments in developing leadership skills to showcase to your manager.

Remember, taking proactive steps towards your goal of moving into a leadership position will demonstrate your initiative and commitment. Keep pushing forward and stay focused on your growth journey.
